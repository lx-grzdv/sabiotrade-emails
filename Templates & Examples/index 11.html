4 Steps To Opening A Trade

No Demo Trades Yet? Let’s Fix That in 4 Steps

Follow these 4 steps to place one clean demo trade today.

Step 1 — Select an asset

In Traderoom, pick any liquid, popular asset (e.g., EUR/USD, Gold, US100).
Open Traderoom & pick an asset

Step 2 — Find an entry signal
BUY idea:

The price is trending up, makes a pullback, and then shows a clear bullish candle from a recent support area.

That’s a classic “textbook” setup many traders use as a starting point.
Find signal in Traderoom

Step 3 — Set a stop-loss (risk limit)
Target risk per trade: max 0.5% of your demo balance

Example: $10,000 demo balance → max loss $50
In Traderoom:

1

Turn on Close at loss

2

Choose Amount

3

Enter $50 (shows as -$50)

4

Confirm the trade

Tip: “Price” and “Distance” are alternative modes — start with Amount for simple risk control.

Result: you know your maximum loss before you click Buy.
View in Traderoom

Step 4 — Set a take-profit target

Aim for a reward-to-risk ratio of at least 1:1, ideally 2:1.

If your stop-loss is set to $50, set take-profit to $50–$100 (1:1 to 2:1).
In Traderoom:

1

Turn on Close at profit

2

Select Amount and enter Amount $50–$100

3

Confirm the trade

After the trade closes, review the outcome — or discuss it with a mentor to understand what was done right or wrong.
