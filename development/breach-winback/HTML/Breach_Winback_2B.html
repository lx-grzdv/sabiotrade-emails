Assessment accounts are hard - especially the first time. The rules are strict by design, and learning them costs something. That's normal.

Position size is everything

Max 0.5% of account per trade. This is the single most important rule. It means you can have 10 losing trades and still be within the drawdown limit.

$20K account -> max $100 risk per trade

$50K account -> max $250 risk per trade

Set this limit before you open any position.

Apply it for 5 consecutive demo sessions before your next attempt.

2

Don't trade news events without a stop-loss

Slippage during volatility can breach daily limits in seconds. Either avoid trading 30 minutes around major news events - or set a hard stop before you enter.

Check the economic calendar before every session.

Mark high-impact news days in advance.

Use a smaller position size on news days if you do trade.

This one habit alone prevents most daily loss limit breaches.
